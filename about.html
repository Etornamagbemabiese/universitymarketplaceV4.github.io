About UNC Marketplace

Etornam Agbemabiese

UNC Chapel Hill Student

Founder & Developer

Hello! I'm Etornam Agbemabiese, a student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. As a Tar Heel, I've experienced firsthand the challenges students face when trying to exchange items on campus.

During my time at UNC, I noticed that students often need to buy, sell, or trade various items - from textbooks and furniture to sports equipment and dorm essentials. However, there wasn't a dedicated platform that made these exchanges easy and secure within our campus community.

Our Mission

UNC Marketplace was created to provide a safe, convenient, and sustainable way for Carolina students to connect and exchange items. Our platform aims to:

  • • Reduce waste by promoting item reuse
  • • Help students save money
  • • Build a stronger campus community
  • • Make exchanges safe and convenient

As a fellow student, I understand the importance of having access to affordable resources while also being environmentally conscious. UNC Marketplace is my way of giving back to the Carolina community and making campus life a little easier for everyone.

This platform is built with the unique needs of UNC students in mind, from the locations we use to the types of items commonly exchanged. It's a project born from my own experiences and the shared experiences of my fellow Tar Heels.

Have questions or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you!
